FAQ

  • Go to the HZ website and login with your username and password. Click on Choose File and select the file you want to print - repeating the step if you need to add more documents. After selecting all documents, tick the Advanced option to choose whether you want double-sided or black & white printing. Choose between the "HZ printer"/"HZ plotter". After requesting the print you must register using your HZ pass on the TouchID next to the multifunctional printer and select "Print". In the menu, select the printer to which you requested the print. The print will be completed only if there is enough credit on your printing account. When everything is completed, press "Stop" on the TouchID, and lastly - "Logout".
